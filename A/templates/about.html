BLASTER

Makes your BLASTing easier!

Blaster is a tool for local BLAST results visualization. Here you are able to compare many VS many nucleotide sequences and store result in EXCEL spreadsheet. All is what you need is to provide query sequence fasta/multifasta file and database sequences in archive or multifasta. You can choose algorithm (blastn, megablast) and sort out hits by size, coverage or identity.
